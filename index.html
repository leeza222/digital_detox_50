Understanding Screen Overuse Effects

Excessive screen time impacts every aspect of your life. Here's how digital overload affects you.

Eye Strain

Prolonged screen exposure causes dry eyes, blurred vision, and headaches affecting your daily comfort.

Sleep Disturbance

Blue light disrupts melatonin production, making it harder to fall asleep and reducing sleep quality.

Stress & Anxiety

Constant notifications and information overload increase cortisol levels and mental exhaustion.

Low Productivity

Digital distractions fragment focus, reducing deep work capability and overall efficiency.
